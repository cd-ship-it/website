Before you arrive

Your first Sunday,
sorted.

Here's exactly what happens when you walk through the door.

1
Park & walk in
Free parking at every campus. Look for our greeters — they're the ones smiling at the door. No check-in required. Just come in.
2
Grab a coffee
Most campuses have a welcome area with coffee and light refreshments. A great place to meet people before the service starts.
3
Worship together
Contemporary music, a practical message from the Bible, and about 75 minutes total. Dress however you're comfortable — we mean that.
4
No pressure. No obligation.
Come, see how it feels, and leave when you're ready. If you love it, we'd love to connect you. If you have questions, we're happy to answer them.

What it's like

Open, alive, and real.

Here's the vibe you can expect every Sunday.

🎵
Worship Style
Contemporary & Heartfelt
Live music that's modern and singable. Not a performance — a room full of people genuinely worshipping together.
📖
Teaching Style
Practical & Bible-Based
Messages that connect Scripture to everyday life. No theological jargon — just honest, applicable truth delivered with warmth.
🌏
Languages
English, Cantonese & Mandarin
Services offered in all three languages across our campuses. Everyone hears the message in the language closest to their heart.
